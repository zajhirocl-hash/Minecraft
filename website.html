üìñ What is Minecraft?

Minecraft is a sandbox video game where players can build, explore, and survive in blocky, procedurally generated worlds. Originally created by Markus "Notch" Persson and now developed by Mojang Studios under Microsoft, Minecraft has become a cultural phenomenon with over 300 million copies sold worldwide.

The game offers complete creative freedom, allowing players to construct elaborate structures, create intricate redstone contraptions, farm resources, and face challenges from various mobs. Whether you're a builder, explorer, engineer, or survivalist, Minecraft provides endless possibilities for entertainment and creativity.
